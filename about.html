About Me.

Currently working as a Freelance designer I’ve worked on projects ranging from websites and print layouts, to exhibits and set designs. I’m a storyteller at heart, with an unbreakable love for engaging my audience (probably comes from that old dream of becoming a broadway actress). A sucker for grand experiences, I strive to create designs that captivate and immerse.
When not behind a screen you will find me absorbing the nearest museum, taking yet another travel excursion, or trying new craft beer (sometimes with cheese!). I’m always looking for a new problem to solve, so if you’ve got a doozy you need a hand with, feel free to contact me directly by clicking here.
