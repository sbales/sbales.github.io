About Me.

I am a multi-skilled visual interaction designer, specializing in digital experiences, graphic treatment, and promotional marketing materials for small businesses and non-profits. Creating projects for business groups ranging from dedicated musicians, to hard-core gamers, and city-tourism to total hijinx-I’m dedicated to helping you promote you or your brand.

Before embarking on my freelance career I spent time as a student and creator of Theatrical Scenic design, often incorporating my skills of visual storytelling in my current projects. When not behind a screen you will find me absorbing culture in the nearest museum, taking yet another travel excursion, or trying new beer. I currently reside in the Springfield, MO area.
