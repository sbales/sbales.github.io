Exhibit Design

Creating spaces that tell stories through graphic interpretation and teach with interaction.

Let me help you're organization develop your best exhibit yet.

01 Research

Diving deep, research brings small details to the surface that help inform more engaging design decisions by connecting directly with the content.

02 Design

Lots of ideas and input from the research stage allow me to develop a graphic, interaction, or 3D design that will really capture the spirit of your exhibit.

03 Finalize

Once approved, I package everything up with a bow and send it for fabrication. I work directly with fabricators to keep you mind at ease.

My Services

I can help you get your exhibit ready to go, from beginning to end. I'll even help you work with the install team to get things just right.


Concept Development
Big picture planning for getting started. We'll come up with the idea and how it fits in your current space.


Identity & Graphics
The visual look and feel. I'll help you develop a palette and the production files to match.


3D Visualization
If you need to see it to believe it, let me help you out. I'll develop a scale model you can share with others before you install.


Interaction Design
Engagement extreme! We can develop a physical or digital interactive to fit your organization's needs.
