Endgame by Samuel Beckett

Client:
Drury University Theatre Department
Directed by:
Dr. Mick Sokol
Project Type:
Theatrical Stage Production
Role:
Scenic Designer

Endgame by Samuel Beckett is a one-act absurdist piece about four characters living in the near future as the world comes to an end. The show ran for 5 nights, with 6 performances in March of 2014. The set reflected the inevitable decay outside. The flooring was modeled after a topography map covered in a chess board giving it a fluid feel that entered into the audience space. The physical attributes of the set, including texture and color, came from studying dilapidated buildings. The fluid lines of the upper walls were to mimic the flooring and give a sense of imaginary space.
