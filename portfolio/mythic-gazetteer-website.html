Founded in 2016, The Mythic Gazetteer strives to bring you action-packed gaming using myth, folklore, and history as primary ingredients. In their first setting, the Blackwood, you play as errant adventurers in a dark, magical forest inspired by German, Asian, and American folktales. I was approached by the owner to create a website theme for them to blog and showcase their games design. Their design needed to showcase their site as professional, fun, and dedicated. Between the use of bright coloring for the main pages and dark tints for The Blackwood, The Mythic Gazetteer has a fully responsive blog theme that they can grow with and will continue to embody their brand.
