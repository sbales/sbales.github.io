MoNatSci Wayfinding

Client:
Missouri Institute of Natural Science
Project Type:
Wayfinding / Environmental Graphic Design
Role:
Volunteer Designer & Interpretative Writer

Missouri Institute of Natural Science is a 100% volunteer driven Natural Science Museum in Springfield Missouri. As the only natural science museum in the area, MoNatSci, caters to everyday patrons, school field trips, cave tours, and even birthday parties. After a walk-through tour and communications with the volunteer staff, I addressed the need for organization and interpretation. I utilized the recently redesigned logo to develop a wayfinding system where each icon & color refers to a specific sector. The museum is currently in implementation phase.
