MariCorpUS Tradeshow Materials

Print & Exhbit Design

Project Details
  • Client: MariCorpUS
  • Conjointly: Red Crow Marketing
  • Location: Shell Knob, Missouri
  • Timeline: 2 Months
  • Completed: July 2016
The Brief

Under the Creative Direction of Red Crow Marketing, I was requested to design trade show marketing materials for MariCorpUS. These included a coffee table book of built marinas, a services brochure, and trade show banner stands.

Design Process

Starting with only images and content, I developed, first, the coffee table book using MariCorpUS brand standards. Following this I was able to furhter develop the services brochure and trade show banner stands. Most of the work completed was photoshopping the images.

Solution

The final design was a 30 page coffee table book and two half-fold tabloid sized brochures. Current materials are being used for in-person marketing and tradeshow settings.

Project Client

MariCorpUS are a marina and boatlift specialists. They design and build boat marinas for lakes, rivers, and ocean front properties. They also sell boat lifts directly to owners and hobbyists.

In Conjunction With

Red Crow Marketing was hatched in 2004 to help businesses with their advertising and marketing needs. Red Crow Marketing isn’t a big company; they're a personal-sized marketing company. Their goal is not to acquire the most clients; their goal is to provide exceptional one-on-one service to the clients they work with. Red Crow Marketing offers a wide variety of services you would typically find only in a large agency.
