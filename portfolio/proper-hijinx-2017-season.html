Client:
Proper Hijinx Productions
Project Type:
Promotional Marketing
Role:

Proper Hijinx Productions is an independent theatre company in Dallas TX, founded by artistic director Stefany Cambra in late 2015. Their goal is to produce unique works that will resonate with the audience, focusing on the art of storytelling. Proper Hijinx Productions also advocates for growth in the theatre community by attending as many outside productions as possible. After completing their logo and website design, Stefany asked me to continue on as their dedicated promotions designer. I have been working with the group since 2015. Below is the 2017 season.
