UK Benchmark Report

Production Graphic Designer
Project Details
  • Client: NAVEX Global
  • Location: Lake Oswego, Oregon
  • Date: October 2016

The Brief

The UK Benchmark Report was produced for the EMEA side of NAVEX Global’s business. Based on reports developed for the US side, I was tasked with determining the exact style of the UK version, including updates to the visual style, iconography, and infographic style. This 12 page report was designed, produced, and implemented over the course of just 15 days.

About The Client

NAVEX Global's enterprise compliance system is changing the way compliance impacts business goals. Their mission is to help organizations protect and defend their people, reputation and bottom line—and help them maintain a resilient, ethical organizational culture that helps repel risk.
