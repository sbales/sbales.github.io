Murder Mystery Logo

Client:
US Actors Company
Project Type:
Logo Design
Role:
Designer

US Actors Company is a Murder Mystery Theatre company out of Dallas TX. I was approached by the owner and artistic director to create a logo that they could use on a variety of print and digital publications and marketing materials. They are developers of modern and classic style stories, very different from the western and police drama styles of their competitors. Although it might seem cliche to do so, I chose to follow a modern Film Noir style--US Actors Company truly embodies this spirit.
