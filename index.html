US Actors Company

US Actors Company is a Murder Mystery Theatre company out of Dallas TX. With a recent turn over in ownership, the company was looking for a re-vamp in their logo design. With several other murder mystery and niche theatre storytelling companies, they really wanted to stand out while still holding true to company standards. I was approached by the owner and artistic director to create a modern murder mystery inspired logo that they could use on a variety of print and digital publications and marketing materials.

Missed Connections

"Missed Connections", a show about love lost and love never had, was produced by Proper Hijinx in conjunction with the FIT festival. I was approached to create a web poster, postcard, bussiness card, and social media advertisements to use in both Proper Hijinx's and FIT's campaigns.

Proper Hijinx Productions

Proper Hijinx is a small theatre company out of Dallas Texas, looking to produce quality works focusing on relevant themes and contemporary issues. The artisitic director, Stefany, approached to me to create a logo and web design which would be relavent both to those watching Proper Hijinx's performances, and those wanting to get involved. Describing the company as young, edgy, and looking to stir up the best kind of trouble. Proper Hijinx is a company which is both youthful and contemporary, and needed a design to reflect that.

Edinlore

As a recent resident to Edinburgh, I found it very fascinating that this city holds so much history; no one is ever told all of it. Most of the more complex stories are left out of your standard tour through the city. Beyond that tour, is a whole slew of histories which are left for the wandering to discover for themselves. Edinlore is a project which will help to make this hidden history more accessible to all, while also allowing the capability of deciding what to learn and what to leave behind. The feature of this project is the story based in location. Using NFC technology tags, each location is used to share a lore story using rich media including: audio, visuals, and interactive elements.

Ambience Edinburgh

Ambience Edinburgh is an android app designed to project the weather of Edinburgh through sounds. The app displays weather patterns through noises associated with it, like holiday music with snow and childrens laughter with sunshine. Using Processing and sound editing software Adobe Audition I was able to develop a simple and immersive storytelling app.

Writer's Museum of Edinburgh

The Writer's Museum of Edinburgh is a historic building in the old town, currently housing the exhibits of 3 major Scottish writers. I undertook this project to rethink and redesign the entire building. The interpretation of the museum has not been changed in nearly 30 years, making this the prime location to completely rethink. This is my re-design of the 4 story building including interactive exhibits.

There's Nae Place like Hame

A propsed exhibiton in response to National Museum of Scotland's Design & Home upcoming exhibition. This exhibition looks at the relation between home and design. Co-designed with one other designer, this project includes an intimate and familiar focus on Scottish Media and how it portrays the “Scottish Home”, as well as the perspective of the Scottish Families on the “Scottish Home”.

Endgame

The show is set in the near future as the world comes to an end. The two characters we meet, might be the last living inhabitants on the planet.
