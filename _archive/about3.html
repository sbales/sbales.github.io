About Me

STEVIE BALES

I'm passionate about design.

From healthcare wayfinding systems to branded corporate environments and everything in-between.

As an experiential designer I work to integrate digital and static systems into the built-environment, creating solutions which aid in creating great visitor experiences. I'm working to change the way we percieve the places we interact with everyday.

My training in Interaction Design and background in exhibit set design has provided me with a broad range of technical expertise in wayfinding, spatial storytelling, environmental graphic design, user experience, and creative digital technology. My multidisciplinary work includes projects in: healthcare, transportation, entertainment, arts & culture, corporate, and academia.

When not behind a screen, you will find me absorbing the nearest museum, traveling across the world or down the street, pretending to be a Settler of Catan, or trying new craft beer. I also play role-playing board games, stop at visitor centers on the way in and out, as well as drink wine... sometimes with cheese!
